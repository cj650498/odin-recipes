Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich (PB&J) is a simple yet iconic snack enjoyed by people of all ages. Combining creamy or crunchy peanut butter with sweet and tangy fruit jelly between two slices of bread, this sandwich is the perfect balance of salty, sweet, and satisfying. Itâ€™s quick to make, requires minimal ingredients, and is perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a snack on the go.

Ingredients

  • Bread:
    • 2 slices of white, whole wheat, or multigrain bread
  • Spreads:
    • 2 tablespoons peanut butter (creamy or crunchy)
    • 2 tablespoons fruit jelly or jam (grape, strawberry, etc.)
  • Optional Additions:
    • 1 tablespoon honey for extra sweetness
    • 1 sliced banana for added texture
    • A sprinkle of cinnamon for extra flavor
